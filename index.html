About Me

At sixteen years old, I began spending (probably too much) time on 'underground' hacking forums. I thought it was extremely intriguing, and had an obsessive thirst for knowledge. I have always been well-informed when it comes to technology, so picking up the hobby was a relatively simple task. Throughout the years, I have grown my skills to that of a working & functional ethical hacker. To perspective employers, I can offer my programming stack and networking/cybersecurity skills. This combination allows me to easily audit and create security-oriented code. I owe much of my skills to the hacking community, and to my uncle, who works in tech himself and, in a way, took me under his wing. I have a working knowledge of UI/UX design, SQL, Kubernetes, PHP, Docker, Git, and architecture/security of various cloud platforms. I am fluid programming in Python, C++, markdown languages, CSS, Javascript, UI/UX design, understanding/utilizing vulnerability attack vectors, establishing SIEM firewalls, networking, among others.
